About Our Club

The UNCG Sewing Club was founded by Bertha Jernigan to bring together students who love sewing, crafting, and creating. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced sewist, we welcome everyone!

Our club meets regularly to work on projects, share tips and techniques, and build a supportive community of makers. We provide a space where creativity flourishes and friendships are stitched together.

What We Do

From learning basic stitches to completing advanced projects, our club offers something for everyone. We host workshops, social sewing sessions, and showcase members' creations.
