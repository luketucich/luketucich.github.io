Past Meetings

Take a look back at our previous events and activities!

Fall Kickoff Meeting August 19, 2025

The UNCG Sewing Club made its debut! Club founder Bertha Jernigan, Vice President Itzel Jaimes, Treasurer Brittany Reyes, and Club Advisor Monika Goel welcomed members to our very first meeting. We introduced the club's mission, accepted new members, and discussed exciting plans for the semester ahead. It was wonderful to see so many students interested in joining our sewing community!

Sock Plushies Workshop September 25, 2025

Members learned how to transform ordinary socks into adorable plushies! This fun and creative project was perfect for beginners and experienced sewers alike. Everyone got to take home their own unique sock plushie creation, from cute animals to creative characters.

Patchwork Making October 9, 2025

We explored the art of patchwork! Working with different fabrics to create custom pieces. This meeting introduced essential skills for future projects and helped members develop their fabric coordination abilities.

Fall Refreshments General Meeting October 23, 2025

A casual social gathering where members enjoyed fall-themed refreshments while working on their individual projects. This relaxed meeting provided a great opportunity for members to share tips, ask questions, and get help with their ongoing sewing endeavors in a comfortable, festive atmosphere.

Tote Bag Prep & Machine Practice November 6, 2025

Members received hands-on instruction in sewing machine operation and prepared for our tote bag project. This practical session covered machine basics, proper threading, stitch selection, and fabric handling. Members practiced their new skills and gained confidence for future machine sewing projects.
